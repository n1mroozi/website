Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 24, 2024

1. Data Collection

RealEasy collects the following data from third-party platforms (e.g., TikTok, Instagram, etc.) as required by their APIs:

  • Access tokens for authentication.
  • Basic profile information, if required by the platform's API.

2. Data Usage

The data collected is used solely for the functionality of RealEasy, such as enabling features that allow you to post or manage content on your connected social media accounts. Your data is not used for any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

RealEasy securely stores access tokens and other necessary data. All data is deleted when it is no longer required for the app's functionality or when you revoke permissions for RealEasy from the respective third-party platform.

4. Data Sharing

RealEasy does not share your data with any third parties. All data is handled in strict compliance with this Privacy Policy and the terms set by the third-party platforms.

5. Your Choices

You have control over the data you share with RealEasy. You can revoke permissions at any time directly from the connected platform's settings (e.g., TikTok, Instagram, etc.). Doing so may limit the app's functionality.
